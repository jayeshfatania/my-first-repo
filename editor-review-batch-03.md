## 1. BATCH OVERVIEW

Batch 03 is the strongest batch to date. Voice consistency is markedly improved across all five personas, walk-name openers are down to borderline cases only, and several descriptions are genuinely excellent — cave-hill-belfast, savernake-forest and valley-of-rocks-lynton in particular. The NI entries land convincingly. No full rewrites are needed.

Three issues require attention:

**1. Priya's "We stumbled" opener — third appearance.**
gibraltar-point-lincolnshire opens with "We stumbled across this one on a rainy October weekend." frensham-common (Batch 01) opened with "We stumbled on this one." penllergare-valley-woods (Batch 02) opened with "We stumbled across this one." The fix was applied to Batch 02. It has recurred in Batch 03. One sentence rewrite required.

**2. Jamie's food/drink omission — pattern across 4 of 5 entries.**
The persona spec states Jamie "Will mention nearby food/drink options even when not asked." Four of five Jamie descriptions in this batch contain no food or drink reference (valley-of-rocks-lynton, blaise-castle-estate, epping-forest-chingford, dovestone-reservoir). Only blaise-castle-estate and epping-forest-chingford are flagged for fixes — valley-of-rocks-lynton has a legitimate structural reason for omission (the walk is explicitly about the drama of the location, and a food mention would break the rhythm), and dovestone-reservoir's omission is acceptable given the shorter entry. Two additions needed.

**3. "Which is a genuine bonus" in lepe-country-park — near-miss of retired Deborah phrase.**
This is Priya's description, not Deborah's, and the phrasing differs ("a genuine bonus" vs "always a bonus"). Not a carry-forward violation. However, the construction is close enough to flag as a minor tidy.

Standing rule confirmations:
- "which is always a bonus" — confirmed absent from all 20 Batch 03 descriptions ✓
- "Ideal for" as Deborah closer — confirmed absent ✓
- "We loved/love this one" as Priya opener — confirmed absent ✓
- Pete imperfections are apostrophe-drops only — confirmed correct throughout ✓
- Morag pub/café requirement — present in all 4 Morag entries ✓

Twelve entries pass without changes. One rewrite required, two additions, one minor tidy.
